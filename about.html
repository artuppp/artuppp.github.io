Me

Arturo Vicente Jaén, Ph.D. Student

I am a Ph.D. student researching real-time systems for human visual optics, combining hardware accelerators and deep learning. I hold a Computer Engineering degree and a master’s in New Technologies in Computer Science from the University of Murcia, where I received top honors and a doctoral scholarship. My thesis focuses on high-performance image processing for applications like pupil tracking and 3D OCT reconstruction, aiming to improve performance and energy efficiency in visual optics using advanced computing techniques.
